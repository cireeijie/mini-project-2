About Us
Bringing the Authentic Flavors of Korea to Your Table

Welcome to Korean Delight Davao, your go-to destination for authentic Korean cuisine in the heart of Davao City. We are a family-owned business committed to providing our customers with delicious, high-quality Korean dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Our menu features a wide variety of traditional Korean dishes, from classic favorites like bibimbap and bulgogi to lesser-known delicacies like jajangmyeon.

At Korean Delight Davao, we pride ourselves on providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our customers. Our friendly staff are always happy to help you choose the perfect dish or answer any questions you may have about our menu.

Whether you're a Korean food enthusiast or a first-time customer, we invite you to come experience the flavors of Korea at Korean Delight Davao. We look forward to serving you soon!
